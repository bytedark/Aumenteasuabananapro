# Design Guidelines: High-Converting Dark Sales Landing Page

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Aggressive conversion-focused sales page inspired by high-performance marketing funnels (ClickFunnels, Leadpages style) with premium dark aesthetic.

## Core Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Pure black (#000000)
- **Accent Color**: Vibrant yellow (#FFD300) for highlights, icons, dividers
- **CTA Buttons**: Neon green (#00FF66) with 1.5s pulsing animation
- **Typography**: White/light gray for readability on black

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, extra-large, aggressive persuasive copy style
- **Subheadlines**: Medium weight, direct and action-oriented
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Hierarchy**: Extreme contrast between headline sizes and body text

### Spacing System
Use Tailwind units: 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 for consistent rhythm throughout

## Layout Structure

### 1. Hero Section (Above Fold)
- Full-width black background
- Extra-large persuasive headline with yellow accents
- Short, direct subheadline
- Prominent video player space (center or right)
- Pulsing green CTA button displaying "R$19,90"
- "Últimas vagas/unidades" urgency text in yellow
- Smooth entrance animations on scroll

### 2. Social Proof Notifications
- Floating notification cards appearing every 10-20 seconds
- Simulate real purchases: "João from SP just purchased!"
- Position: Bottom-right or top-right corner
- Slide-in animation with auto-dismiss

### 3. Product Proof Section
- Before/After comparisons (side-by-side on desktop, stacked mobile)
- Bold mini-headlines in yellow
- Data points, statistics, effectiveness metrics
- Clear visual evidence with images/graphics

### 4. Persuasion Sections (Multiple)
Each section with:
- Aggressive headline
- Bullet points with yellow markers
- Supporting copy in emotional, persuasive tone
- Sections include: "Por que funciona?", "O que você vai receber", "Oportunidade limitada"

### 5. Video Section
- Large responsive video player
- Minimal dark-themed controls
- CTA button directly below player (pulsing green)
- Compelling call-to-action copy above player

### 6. Testimonials Section
- Customer photos with testimonials
- Screenshot evidence
- Real comment-style formatting
- 2-3 column grid on desktop, stacked mobile

### 7. Trust & Guarantee Elements
- Security badges (verified, secure payment)
- Unconditional guarantee seal
- Payment method icons (PIX, credit cards)
- Yellow borders/highlights around badges

### 8. Offer & Urgency Section
- Massive "R$19,90" display in green/yellow
- Countdown timer with animated numbers
- "Limited spots available" messaging
- Pulsing green purchase buttons
- Payment options clearly visible

### 9. Footer
- Essential links (terms, privacy, support) in subtle gray
- Final pulsing green CTA button
- Minimal design, dark theme continuation

## Component Specifications

### Pulsing CTA Buttons
- Green (#00FF66) background
- 1.5-second pulse animation (scale + opacity)
- Always visible, never hidden
- Appear in hero, after each major section, video section, footer
- Bold text with high contrast

### Countdown Timer
- Large animated numbers
- Yellow accents for emphasis
- Hours:Minutes:Seconds format
- Positioned near main offer

### Section Dividers
- Thin yellow lines or geometric shapes
- Subtle separation between major sections
- Maintain dark aesthetic flow

## Responsive Behavior
- **Mobile**: Single column, stacked sections, larger touch targets for CTAs
- **Desktop**: Multi-column where appropriate (testimonials, features)
- **Video**: Responsive aspect ratio (16:9), full-width on mobile
- **Notifications**: Adjust position for mobile (bottom-center)

## Animations & Interactions
- Smooth scroll-triggered entrance animations (fade-up)
- Pulsing effect on ALL green CTAs (continuous 1.5s loop)
- Countdown timer number transitions
- Social proof notification slide-ins
- Minimal hover states (maintain dark theme)

## Images
- **Hero Image/Mockup**: Product visualization or lifestyle imagery positioned alongside video player
- **Before/After**: Visual proof images with clear comparison
- **Testimonial Photos**: Customer headshots for authenticity
- **Trust Badges**: Security seals, payment method icons
- All images optimized for dark background with appropriate contrast

## Copywriting Tone
Aggressive, persuasive, emotional, urgent. Use scarcity, authority, social proof, and desire triggers throughout. Direct, confident language with strong CTAs.
